THIEFABLE ITEMS DOC

Thief becomes obtainable as a TM upon reaching Slateport, the following doc lists the earliest instance of all available items. No repeats are listed. However, certain
Pokemon can learn Thief/Covet prior to obtaining the TM. Here's a list of these pokemon with the level they learn their respective move:

Zigzagoon/Linoone - Covet @ Lv. 15
Fennekin - Can have Magician as an ability if you're brave enough to go for the Horde encounter
Lillipup - Covet @ Lv. 12
Purrloin - Thief @ Lv. 1
Solosis - Trick @ Lv. 5
Slakoth - Covet @ Lv. 18
Pumpkaboo - Trick @ Lv. 1
Tepig - Covet @ Lv. 13

Note that most of these items only have a 5% chance to be held unless stated otherwise.

The following Mega Stones become available after Groudon/Kyogre storyline is completed, and can't be bought in shops. All other Mega Stones should be available through shops or found on the ground as you progress in the story:
*Sceptilite/Blazikenite/Swampertite - Steven gives you the Mega Stone for your starter shortly before Winona. The remaining two can be bought off an NPC that appears
on Route 114 outside Fallarbor Town after beating Primal Groudon/Kyogre
*Venusaurite - Route 119 in front of the house at beginning of route
*Charizardite - X is in Fiery Path, requires Strength to access. Y is in Scorched Slab in Heatran room
*Alakazite - SW corner of Slateport Market. Available as early as post Brawly, but very easy to miss so listing it here!
*Kangaskhanite - Pacifidlog Town, behind town sign. Requires Surf
*Aerodactylite - Meteor Falls, requires Surf + Waterfall
*Mewtwonite - X is in Littleroot Town, Y is in front of Pokemon League
*Scizorite - Petalburg Woods, south of Moss Rock
*Houndoominite - Lavaridge Town next to sand baths, don't forget to pick up the Togepi egg/Kecleon encounter in the springs while you're here!
*Tyranitarite - Jagged Pass, northeast of southern entrance
*Gardevoirite - Gift from Wanda for smashing rocks in Rusturf Tunnel
*Altarianite - Lisia fan club, need Altaria in party - available as soon as arriving in Lilycove, but also easy to miss!
*Salamencite - held by wild Shelgon/Salamence (found in Meteor Falls, need Surf + Waterfall)
*Diancite - held by wild Diancie (100% chance), found in Mirage Caves
*Latiasite (RR)/Latiosite (SS) - post Delta Episode unfortunately, though wild Latios/Latias in Mirage Island spots can hold Soul Dew!
*Abomasite - northeast area of Berry Master's garden
