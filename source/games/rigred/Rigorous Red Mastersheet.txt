Notes:
-ALL TRAINERS HAVE RANDOMIZED NATURES, PLEASE CHECK THE CALC TO SEE WHICH NATURE EACH MON HAS
-ALL BOSSES (Rivals, Gym Leaders, Rocket Executives/Leaders, E4 Members and bosses that guard items) HAVE 31 IVS IN ALL STATS, REGULAR TRAINERS (including minibosses, Koichi and Victory Road trainers) ALL HAVE 25 IVS IN ALL STATS
-read the Pokemon changes doc, there’s a ton of type and stat changes so make sure you’re aware of those
-major bosses are bolded and underlined, minor bosses are underlined, minibosses are found at the end of routes/areas 
-Rock Types do not get a SpDef boost in Sandstorm like Gen 4+
-healing items on trainers like potions and full heals are removed
-The only fishing rod is the old rod
-HMs are now deletable
-if a route has fog in it, that means you can no longer fly while on that route
-at the end of each gauntlet (route 15, route 17 and route 20), you have to pick up the porta pc again since it gets taken away at the start of each of them
-all trainers have the same AI as gym leaders 
-currently weather ball is bugged in the calc
